## Instructions:

1. **Copy your `.mp4` video file** to this folder
2. **Rename it to:** `default.mp4`
3. **Run:** `Add-Video.bat` to verify
4. **Rebuild QOR Desktop**:
   - Use `Simple-Rebuild.bat` in the parent directory
   - Or manually: `cd build && cmake --build . --config Release`
5. **Launch QOR** and enjoy your custom background!

---

**Recommended Video Format:**
- **MP4** with H.264 codec
- **1920x1080** resolution (or higher)
- **30fps** framerate
- **10-60 seconds** duration (loops seamlessly)
- **No audio** needed (will be muted)

**Example conversion with FFmpeg:**
```bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -c:v libx264 -crf 23 -preset medium -s 1920x1080 -r 30 -an default.mp4
```
